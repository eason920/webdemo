Japanese writer Haruki Murakami has written about many subjects in his career, but he rarely mentions his father in his works. However, Murakami, now 70, has recently disclosed that his father had served as a soldier in the Imperial Japanese Army in China.
日本作家村上春樹在他職業生涯中書寫過許多題材，但他鮮少在作品中提到他的父親，然而現年70歲的村上最近在散文中透露他的父親曾以日本軍隊士兵身份在中國服役。
